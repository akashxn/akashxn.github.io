Maximize Profits for EV Battery Provider using K means

Calculating incentive and penalty based on user's driving behaviour. This tool can be used by EV battery providers to judge their customers and charge them accordingly, resulting in 15-20% profits. K Means was implemented from scratch without using sklearn. Expectation Maximization was used to form clusters of drivers to assign them Incentive, Normal, Low Penalty or High Penalty.
